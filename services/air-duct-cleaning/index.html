Our Flagship Service

Why St. Louis Homes Need Professional Duct Cleaning

Every time your HVAC runs, air pulls through the duct system and into every room. If those ducts are full of dust, pet dander, mold, allergens, or construction debris you're breathing every bit of it. Professional duct cleaning removes the buildup at the source, so your home actually breathes clean.

Our St. Louis climate is especially hard on air quality humid summers grow mold spores, dry winters fill the air with dust, and our springs and falls send pollen straight into return vents. That's why most homeowners we serve come back every few years for another cleaning. The dust builds up faster than you'd think.

When You Need It

When Should You Schedule Air Duct Cleaning?

The National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA) recommends professional cleaning every 3 to 7 years. Schedule sooner if any of these apply:

  • You see visible dust collecting around the vents or on furniture shortly after dusting
  • You have pets that shed
  • Family members suffer from allergies, asthma, or respiratory issues
  • You've recently completed a renovation or moved into a previously occupied home
  • You notice musty odors when the HVAC runs
  • The HVAC system is running longer than it used to or your bills have crept up
  • It's been more than 5 years since the last professional cleaning

Not sure where you stand? Call us. We'll ask a few honest questions and tell you whether you need cleaning now or could wait another year. We'd rather earn a customer for life than push a job that isn't ready.

Quick Answers

Common Questions About Air Duct Cleaning

How long does the cleaning take?

A typical 2,000-square-foot home takes 2 to 4 hours. Larger homes (3,000+ sq ft) or homes with two HVAC systems may take longer. We give a realistic time estimate when we provide your quote.

How often should I have it done?

NADCA recommends every 3 to 7 years for most homes. Pets, allergies, recent renovations, or musty odors mean you should clean more often. If it's been more than 5 years, it's time.

Will it make a mess in my house?

No. We use HEPA-filtered industrial vacuums that capture contaminants inside the equipment. We protect floors and work areas, and we leave each room as clean as or cleaner than we found it.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Yes, an adult should be home. We need access to every room with vents and to the air handler. We work quietly so you can continue with your day.

What does it cost?

Cost depends on home size, number of vents, condition of the ductwork, and whether you have one or two HVAC units. We give a free written estimate before any work begins. Call (314) 718-3632 for a quote tailored to your home.
