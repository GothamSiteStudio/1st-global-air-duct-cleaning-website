Commercial Service

Commercial-Grade Cleaning for Commercial Buildings

Commercial HVAC systems run more hours, move more air, and serve more people than residential systems which is exactly why their ducts get dirty faster. We bring the same family-owned care to commercial work that we do to homes, but with industrial equipment and scheduling that fits around your operations.

Property Types We Serve

  • Offices From single-suite tenants to multi-floor headquarters
  • Restaurants Kitchen exhaust separate; dining-area ducts cleaned to the level your customers expect
  • Retail stores After-hours service so we don't disrupt customers
  • Schools & daycares Indoor air quality is critical for children and staff
  • Medical & dental offices Cleaner air supports infection control
  • Small commercial buildings Up to ~25,000 sq ft is our sweet spot
  • Property managers One contact for multiple properties across the metro

Why Commercial Cleaning Is Different

Commercial systems aren't just bigger they're more complex. Multi-zone air handlers, longer duct runs, and rooftop units (RTUs) all require different equipment and approaches. We bring industrial HEPA-vacuum systems, multi-tech crews, and the experience to work safely on commercial roofs and mechanical rooms.

What's Included

  • Full system walk-through and written estimate before work begins
  • Pre-cleaning inspection with photos and video documentation
  • All supply and return ducts cleaned
  • Diffusers, registers, and grilles individually hand-cleaned
  • Air handler unit (AHU) cabinet vacuumed where accessible
  • Coil cleaning and sanitization available as add-on
  • After-hours and weekend scheduling at no extra charge
  • Final walk-through and documentation for your records

Scheduling Around Your Business

We work after hours, on weekends, and overnight when needed at no extra charge for most jobs. Restaurant cleanings typically happen between close and open. Retail jobs run after hours so customers see nothing. Office work is scheduled to a window that fits your team. The goal: zero disruption.

Documentation You Can Use

Every commercial job comes with photo and video documentation, before-and-after records, and a written report. If you need it for tenants, building management, insurance, or compliance we provide it.
