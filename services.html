Service #1

Air Duct Cleaning

Our flagship service deep cleaning of your entire HVAC duct system to remove dust, dander, allergens, mold spores, and debris that have built up over the years.

What's Included

  • Pre-cleaning inspection We use a camera to inspect your ductwork and document the current condition
  • Supply & return air ducts Every accessible duct in your system
  • Registers, grilles & diffusers Hand-cleaned and vacuumed
  • Air handler unit The blower compartment, where accessible
  • HEPA-filtered industrial vacuum Captures contaminants without releasing them back into your home
  • Rotary brush agitation Dislodges stubborn buildup that vacuums alone can't remove
  • Final inspection & walkthrough We show you the difference, with before/after photos

How Long Does It Take?

A typical 2,000-square-foot home takes 2 to 4 hours. Larger homes (3,000+ sq ft) or homes with two HVAC systems may take longer. We'll give you a realistic time estimate when we provide your quote.

When Should You Get It Done?

The National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA) recommends professional cleaning every 3 to 7 years. Consider scheduling sooner if:

  • You see visible dust collecting around vents
  • You have pets that shed
  • Family members suffer from allergies or asthma
  • You've recently completed renovations or moved into a new home
  • You notice musty odors when the HVAC runs

Service #2

Dryer Vent Cleaning

The single most important fire-prevention task for your home and most people skip it. Annual dryer vent cleaning saves lives, money, and dryer machines.

Why This Service Saves Lives

According to the U.S. Fire Administration, dryer fires cause nearly 2,900 home fires each year, resulting in injuries, deaths, and over $35 million in property damage. The leading cause? Lint buildup in the dryer vent.

Warning Signs You Need Cleaning

  • Clothes take longer than one cycle to dry
  • Dryer feels excessively hot to the touch
  • Burning smell when running the dryer
  • Lint visible around the dryer or outside vent
  • It's been more than a year since your last cleaning

What's Included

  • Full vent line cleaning From dryer to outside termination
  • Lint removal High-powered vacuum & mechanical brush
  • Outside vent cap inspection Often blocked by lint, birds, or debris
  • Connection check Ensure proper sealing at all joints
  • Airflow test Verify proper performance after cleaning

How Long Does It Take?

Typical dryer vent cleaning takes 30 to 60 minutes. Longer vent runs (common in newer construction) or vents with significant blockages may take more time.
