FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about air duct cleaning in St. Louis.

The National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA) recommends professional air duct cleaning every 3 to 7 years. However, you should consider more frequent cleanings if you have:

  • Pets that shed hair or dander
  • Family members with allergies, asthma, or respiratory conditions
  • Recent renovations, remodeling, or new construction
  • Visible dust around vents or musty odors
  • Smokers in the home

A typical residential air duct cleaning takes 2 to 4 hours, depending on the size of your home, number of vents and registers, and the condition of your ductwork. Larger homes (3,000+ sq ft) may take longer. Dryer vent cleaning is faster usually 30 to 60 minutes.
